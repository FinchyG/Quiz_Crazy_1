Science and Nature Medium 1

Questions

1: What two chemical elements make up carbon monoxide?

Carbon and oxygen

2: What insect-eating plant species has the scientific name Dionaea muscipula?

Venus Flytrap

3: In computer science, how many bits make up a byte?

Eight

4: What is the chemical symbol for the chemical element sodium?

Na

5: What is the largest living species of fish in the world?

Whale shark

6: The Beaufort scale measures the intensity of what phenomenon?

Wind

7: The asteroid belt mainly lies between what two planets in the Solar System?

Mars and Jupiter

8: Granite is which of the three types of rock found on Earth?

Igneous

9: Brass is an alloy of what two metals?

Copper and zinc

10: What three species of snake are native to the United Kingdom?

Adder, grass snake and smooth snake
