Science and Nature Easy 1

Questions

1: What two chemical elements make up water?

Hydrogen and oxygen

2: What nut is produced by an oak tree?

Acorn

3: How many grams make up a kilogram?

1,000

4: What is the chemical symbol for the chemical element carbon?

C

5: Kiwi birds are native to what country?

New Zealand

6: Ohms and volts measure the resistance and potential of what phenomenon?

Electricity

7: What planet orbits closest to the Sun?

Mercury

8: At how many degrees Celsius does water boil at sea level?

100

9: Bronze is an alloy of what two metals?

Copper and tin

10: Which of the Earth's poles are penguins native to?

South Pole (Antartica)
